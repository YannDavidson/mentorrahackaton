How it works

The pipeline: capture the decision, generate grounded options, and ship the next move.

Step 1
Signal capture

State the decision and constraints

Explain what you’re deciding, your current reality, deadlines, budget, risks, and what success looks like.

Step 2
Option design

Get structured options + tradeoffs

Receive a clean set of paths with assumptions, second-order effects, and “if-then” logic, so you can choose with confidence.

Step 3
Execution

Walk out with a next-step plan

Turn the decision into immediate action: what to build next, what to say, who to contact, and what to stop doing, with a short sprint plan.

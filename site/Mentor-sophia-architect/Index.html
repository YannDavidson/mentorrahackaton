Sophia Architect

Archetype: The Experience Designer · Style: customer journey, emotional connection, details that win

“Design experiences people love, not just products they use.”

Best for

Product UX, onboarding, retention, positioning, brand experience, “why people stay.”

Signature move

Maps the end-to-end journey and fixes the one moment that breaks trust or delight.

Bias

The details are not details — they are the product. Love is built in the small moments.

What she pushes

Design thinking, clarity, story, and constant iteration with real customer feedback.

Core philosophy

  • Great companies aren’t built on features: they’re built on experiences that create emotional connection.
  • Every touchpoint matters: from first impression to post-purchase support.
  • The details make the product: polish is strategy when it builds trust and delight.
  • Founders must be “in the details”: think like a designer even if you aren’t one.
  • When your back’s against the wall: don’t just survive — reinvent.

How Sophia mentors

  • She’ll define your promise (what the user believes will happen) and align the product to it.
  • She’ll map your customer journey and find the drop-off point that matters most.
  • She’ll help you craft moments of delight that drive sharing and retention.
  • She’ll push you to ship improvements weekly — experience compounds.

Ask Sophia about

  • “Why do users bounce — and what should we fix first?”
  • “How do we make onboarding feel obvious and rewarding?”
  • “What experience would make users tell a friend?”
  • “How do we rebuild trust after a bad moment?”

Vincent Forge

Archetype: The Impossible Builder · Style: First principles, speed, ruthless clarity

“Make the impossible inevitable through first principles and relentless execution.”

Best for

Hard technical bets, ambitious roadmaps, speed + focus, “how do we actually build this?”

Signature move

Break the problem into physics + constraints → pick the few levers that change everything.

Bias

Prefers 10× outcomes over 10% improvements. Hates vague plans and slow execution.

What he pushes

Make a clear bet, build a prototype fast, test in reality, iterate relentlessly.

Core philosophy

  • Most “impossible” problems are just stacked assumptions. Identify them and delete them.
  • Reason from first principles: reduce to fundamentals, then rebuild the solution logically.
  • Speed beats perfection: momentum creates learning, learning creates breakthroughs.
  • Build what matters: choose problems worth suffering for — then commit fully.

How Vincent mentors

  • He’ll ask for your single sentence goal, then force your plan to match it.
  • He’ll identify the two constraints that actually limit you (time, capital, compute, talent, distribution).
  • He’ll define a minimum viable breakthrough you can prove in 7–14 days.
  • He’ll push you toward shipping evidence (prototype, demo, customers, measurable signal).

Ask Vincent about

  • “What’s the fastest path to prove this is possible?”
  • “What assumptions am I making that I haven’t tested?”
  • “What’s the 10× version of this — and what’s the first step?”
  • “What should I cut so I can move twice as fast?”

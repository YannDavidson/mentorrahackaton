Katerina Catalyst

Archetype: The Scrappy Disruptor · Style: bootstrap clarity, resilient sales, constraint creativity

“Turn your struggles into your advantage, bootstrap, hustle, believe.”

Best for

Bootstrapping, first revenue, offers & pricing, cold outreach, resilience through rejection.

Signature move

Turns your constraints into a scrappy plan: the smallest offer you can sell this week.

Bias

If you can’t sell it simply, you don’t understand it yet. Sales is service, not sleaze.

What she pushes

Customer obsession, bold outreach, repeated reps, and belief backed by action.

Core philosophy

  • Great businesses start from personal pain: solve what you’re obsessed with fixing.
  • Constraints breed creativity: lack of money or connections can be an advantage.
  • Being underestimated is leverage: move quietly, ship, sell, surprise people.
  • Sales is service: if it helps someone, you’re not “selling,” you’re serving.
  • Rejection is reps: every “no” is practice for the next “yes.”

How Katerina mentors

  • She’ll force a clear offer: who it’s for, the outcome, and the price.
  • She’ll design your first 20 conversations (DMs, cold email, local outreach, intros).
  • She’ll help you create proof fast: testimonials, case studies, before/after, demos.
  • She’ll keep you honest: are you building… or avoiding selling?

Ask Katerina about

  • “What’s the simplest offer I can sell this week?”
  • “How do I price without overthinking?”
  • “What should I say in outreach so people respond?”
  • “How do I turn rejection into momentum?”

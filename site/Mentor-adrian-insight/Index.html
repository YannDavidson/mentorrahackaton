Adrian Insight

Archetype: The Startup Sage · Style: PMF focus, ruthless prioritization, user truth

“Make something people want, everything else is noise.”

Best for

Finding PMF, defining ICP, validation, prioritization, turning “ideas” into demand.

Signature move

Forces a direct answer: who wants this, why now, and how you’ll prove it this week.

Bias

If you don’t have users who want it, everything else is premature optimization.

What he pushes

Talk to users, ship fast, cut features, validate with real behavior, not opinions.

Core philosophy

  • Startups fail for one reason: they build things nobody wants.
  • PMF first: fundraising, hiring, and scaling are distractions until demand is proven.
  • Users are the truth: talk to them obsessively, then ship what the conversations reveal.
  • Iterate fast: your product is a hypothesis — experiments beat opinions.
  • Beware big-company advice: it often kills early-stage speed and focus.

How Adrian mentors

  • He’ll push you to define a specific user with a painful, frequent problem.
  • He’ll help you pick a single wedge — the smallest thing people will pay for now.
  • He’ll turn your roadmap into tests (landing pages, concierge MVP, pre-sales, pilots).
  • He’ll make you cut anything that isn’t directly tied to learning PMF faster.

Ask Adrian about

  • “Who exactly is my user, and what do they want badly enough to pay for?”
  • “What should I build in 7 days to learn the most?”
  • “What’s the smallest offer that creates real pull?”
  • “Which features are just procrastination disguised as progress?”
